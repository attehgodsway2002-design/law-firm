about us

Holly Powell

Holly Powell, LLB (Hons), LLM, practices law in Dornoch, Scotland, with a special focus on criminal defence, catastrophic personal injury, and family law. Her broad experience across both Scottish and UK courts, combined with her expertise in alternative dispute resolution, makes her an invaluable advocate for individuals and business clients alike.

Holly holds a Bachelor of Laws with Honours from the University of Edinburgh, a Master of Laws specialising in dispute resolution, and a postgraduate Diploma in Legal Practice from the Law Society of Scotland. During her studies she was elected as Student Representative to the Faculty of Law and received commendations for excellence in advocacy and clinical legal practice. With over 30 years at the bar, Holly is widely respected for her strategic thinking, compassionate client care, and unwillingness to accept anything less than the best possible outcome. She is known for fighting hard — and for being there personally at every step of your case.

The Firm

An award-recognised and highly regarded Scottish law firm with national reach, Holly Powell Law is built on the belief that problem-solving is at the heart of exceptional legal service. Whether the client is a large corporate or a private individual, every matter is approached with the same rigour and dedication.

Our strong reputation for professionalism and deep knowledge of the areas of law we practise sets us apart. Holly's personal approach means clients are never passed around — they deal with her directly, from the first consultation through to resolution. Our mission is to deliver the finest legal advice and representation, combined with an exceptional standard of care that is genuinely tailored to each client's unique personal or commercial circumstances.
