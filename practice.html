How Can We Help You

Practice Area

Family Law

We represent clients in family court proceedings and in related negotiations, and draft all important legal documentation. Whether you are facing divorce, financial disputes, or matters relating to children and custody, Holly approaches every family case with both legal precision and genuine compassion — helping you move forward with clarity and confidence.

Criminal Defence

One mistake should not ruin your life. Holly works tirelessly to minimise the impact of criminal charges — whether you are facing a minor offence or a serious allegation. From the moment of arrest through to trial and, if necessary, appeal, you will have a resolute and knowledgeable advocate standing firmly in your corner at every stage of the process.

External Counsel Services

On-demand, in-house legal counsel for businesses of all sizes. Holly Powell Law handles day-to-day commercial legal matters, acting as your senior solicitor on a flexible and cost-effective basis — at a fraction of the cost of maintaining in-house legal staff. Ideal for growing businesses that need expert guidance without the overhead.

Juvenile Crimes

We work with young people who have come into contact with the criminal justice system, with the clear goal that mistakes made as a minor will not shadow a young person into adult life. Holly advocates strongly for outcomes that protect the future of young offenders while holding their best interests at the centre of every decision.

Personal Injury

Innocent parties are all too often harmed as a result of someone else's negligence or recklessness. Holly helps victims of accidents, medical negligence, and workplace injuries pursue the full and fair compensation they are entitled to — supporting you throughout the process and giving you the best possible chance of a successful recovery.

Fraud

Penalties for fraud-related charges can be severe and long-lasting. Holly works with clients to challenge the evidence, protect their reputation, and pursue the best available resolution. Whether you face allegations of financial fraud, identity theft, or related offences, you can expect a thorough and determined defence strategy.

Holly Powell Law Firm

Holly Powell Law prides itself on doing things differently. When you book a consultation, you speak directly with Holly — never a paralegal or a salesperson. We do things the right way, not the easy way.
